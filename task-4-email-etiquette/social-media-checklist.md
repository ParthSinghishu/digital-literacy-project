✅ Do’s

* Think before posting—assume everything is permanent
* Keep profiles professional (especially LinkedIn)
* Verify information before sharing
* Respect others’ opinions and privacy
* Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication



❌ Don’ts

* Don’t share personal/sensitive information publicly
* Don’t engage in online arguments or toxic discussions
* Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content
* Don’t spread misinformation or unverified news
* Don’t ignore your digital footprint (future employers check it)
